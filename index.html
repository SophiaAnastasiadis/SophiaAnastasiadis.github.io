Jane Doe

Author | Editor | Creative

Sophia Anastasiadis is a 19 year old emerging writer from Melbourne, Australia. She is currently in her final year of a Bachelor of Creative Writing at the Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology (RMIT). She is looking to share her stories with the world, especially the series of novels she has been working on for over two years, titled "The Heart of the Sun" trilogy.

Sophia loves all things fantasy, sci-fi and romance and she is a member of the LGBTQIA+ community. She is passionate about fandoms and enjoys making fan edits/fanvids of her favourite shows, movies and books. She also enjoys creative colouring and is currently working through the Sunny Days Colouring "Cosy Colouring" workbooks. You can find info about all of her creative projects (big, small and in between) on her creative Instagram, below.
